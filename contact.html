Questions

Frequently Asked.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Ride Forward Foundation is a program of The Genius Foundation, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Contributions are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Where does my money go?

Every contribution directly supports the cost of getting and keeping kids on the track — memberships, race fees, bikes, helmets, protective gear, and the mentorship and development programming around each rider.

Who is eligible for The Starting Gate Program?

Our program is designed for young people who want to experience BMX and mountain bike racing but face financial barriers to participating. Reach out using the form above to learn about applying.

How can my business become a sponsor?

We offer tiered sponsorship packages from $500 to $10,000+, each with brand visibility and tangible rider impact. Visit our Sponsor page or contact us to get started.

Can I volunteer?

Absolutely — coaches, mentors, and event volunteers are the heart of our community. Select "Volunteering" in the form above and tell us how you'd like to help.
