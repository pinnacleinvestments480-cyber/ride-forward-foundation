About Us

We Believe Talent Is Everywhere. Opportunity Isn't.

Ride Forward Foundation exists to keep the door to BMX and mountain bike racing open for every child — regardless of their financial circumstances.

Our Story

A sport that changes lives — out of reach for too many.

BMX and mountain bike racing teach lessons that last a lifetime: how to fall down and get back up, how to set a goal and chase it, how to show up and do the work. But for many families, the cost of entry quietly closes the door before a child ever reaches the gate.

Ride Forward Foundation was created to change that — removing barriers and surrounding each young rider with the support they need to thrive on the track and in life.

Mission

To use BMX and mountain bike racing as a vehicle to help young people build resilience, responsibility, confidence, healthy habits, and meaningful community connections while creating opportunities that prepare them for success in life.

Vision

A future where every child has the opportunity to discover their potential and experience the life-changing benefits of BMX and mountain bike racing — regardless of their financial circumstances.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Access for All

Talent and determination are everywhere; opportunity is not. We remove the barriers that keep deserving kids off the track.

Resilience Through Racing

Falling down and getting back up is the heart of the sport — and of growing up. Setbacks are never the end of the story.

Responsibility & Discipline

Showing up, caring for equipment, and honoring commitments build habits that carry far beyond the track.

Confidence & Self-Belief

Every gate drop is a small act of courage. We help riders discover they are capable of more than they imagined.

Healthy Habits for Life

Physical activity, nutrition, rest, and goal setting are foundations we instill early, so they last a lifetime.

Community & Belonging

No rider succeeds alone. We build a network of riders, families, coaches, mentors, and supporters.

Integrity & Stewardship

As a program of an established 501(c)(3), we treat every dollar as a responsibility, with full transparency.

Our Signature Program

The Starting Gate Program: getting kids to the line — and helping them thrive.

Many promising young riders never get the chance to compete — not for lack of heart, but because the cost of entry is out of reach. The Starting Gate Program replaces those barriers with a structured pathway of support.

  • Scholarships for memberships and race entry fees.
  • Equipment — race-ready bikes, helmets, and protective gear.
  • Mentorship & coaching from experienced volunteers.
  • Development workshops on resilience, nutrition, and life skills.
  • A community of belonging for riders and their families.
