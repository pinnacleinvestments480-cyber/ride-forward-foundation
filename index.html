Our Mission

Building Stronger Kids Through BMX & Mountain Bike Racing.

We use the discipline, excitement, and community of racing as a vehicle to help young people build resilience, responsibility, confidence, healthy habits, and meaningful connections that prepare them for success in life.

Three Pillars

Three Ways We Move Kids Forward.

Access

Removing financial barriers so more youth can participate in BMX and mountain bike racing — memberships, race fees, bikes, and gear.

Development

Helping youth build resilience, responsibility, confidence, healthy habits, and goal setting — on the track and in life.

Community

Creating meaningful connections among riders, families, coaches, mentors, sponsors, and supporters who lift each other up.

The Barrier

For too many families, the race ends before it begins.

Between memberships, race fees, a race-ready bike, a helmet, and protective gear, the cost of getting a child to the starting gate can climb well past a thousand dollars — long before a single race is run.

Our Signature Program

The Starting Gate Program

Our flagship initiative does one thing well: get more kids to the starting gate, then help them thrive once they're there.

  • Scholarships covering memberships and race entry fees.
  • Equipment support — bikes, helmets, and protective gear.
  • Mentorship & coaching from experienced volunteers.
  • Development workshops on goal setting and healthy habits.
